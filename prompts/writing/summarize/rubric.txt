# writing-summarize rubric

Score each dimension 1–3. **Pass = average ≥ 2.0 and clear lift over the control.**

## Dimensions

**coverage** — Does the summary keep the main point, the key reasons, the important figures, and any stated constraints?
- 1: Misses the main point or a load-bearing constraint
- 2: Keeps the core but drops a figure, reason, or constraint
- 3: All load-bearing elements present

**faithfulness** — Does it avoid adding facts, opinions, or conclusions the text doesn't contain?
- 1: Adds recommendations or facts not in the source
- 2: One borderline inference presented as fact
- 3: Strictly faithful to the source

**output_discipline** — Is the response only the summary, with no preamble or commentary, and not padded beyond the source's length?
- 1: Includes preamble, commentary, or balloons a short note into an essay
- 2: Mostly clean but includes a label or wrapper
- 3: The summary and nothing else
