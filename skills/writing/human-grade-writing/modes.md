# Mode Selection

Choose the level of rhetorical force based on the format.

## Product Essay

Use clear claims, concrete mechanisms, restrained cadence, and practical endings. Avoid heavy repetition unless the piece is meant to be polemical.

For this repository, also apply `AGENTS.md`: product judgment, named examples, evals, permissions, release gates, and outcomes over output theater.

## LinkedIn Post

Use sharper openings, compressed paragraphs, and one or two memorable lines. Avoid sounding like a keynote unless the subject has public weight.

## Speech

Use more repetition, parallel structure, direct address, and cadence. The ear matters more. The writing should survive being spoken aloud.

## Civic Essay

Use moral restraint, institutional context when appropriate, and earned uplift. Avoid slogans unless the slogan compresses a fully developed argument. See [civic-prose.md](civic-prose.md).

## Memo

Use the least ornament. Prefer explicit reasoning, tradeoffs, and decisions. A memorable phrase is useful only if it improves alignment.

## Personal Essay

Use concrete memory, observation, and emotional honesty. Avoid turning the self into a symbol too quickly. Let the particular remain particular long enough to feel real.

Do not invent personal facts. Use only what the user supplied.
